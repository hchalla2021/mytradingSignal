## Reading the Boxes

### Main Prediction (Top Purple Box)

| Indicator | Meaning | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **UP** | Price likely up 5-15 min | Add to longs, trail stop up |
| **DOWN** | Price likely down 5-15 min | Add to shorts, trail stop down |
| **REVERSAL** | Reversal warning | Exit or reduce position |
| **Confidence** | 30-85% conviction | Higher = stronger signal |
| **Risk: RED** | HIGH (revers risk) | Reduce position immediately |
| **Risk: AMBER** | MEDIUM (caution) | Monitor closely, tight stops |
| **Risk: GREEN** | LOW (safe) | Add position on dips |

### Four Analysis Boxes

#### Box 1: Positioning Momentum
```
Positioning: ACCELERATING UP · improving
```

| Status | Interpretation | Trader Action |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| ACCELERATING UP | Fresh longs entering fast | BUY dips |
| ACCELERATING DOWN | Fresh shorts entering fast | SELL rallies |
| DECELERATING | Slowing momentum | Tighten stops, watch break |
| STABLE | No new momentum | Wait for breakout |

#### Box 2: Confidence Velocity
```
Confidence: improving (+2.1% per cycle)
```

| Status | Interpretation | Trader Action |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| improving | Institutions more bullish | Follow the trend |
| rapidly improving | Sudden bullish shift | Add to longs |
| eroding | Institutions losing conviction | Reduce position |
| rapidly eroding | 🚨 BULL TRAP WARNING | EXIT IMMEDIATELY |
| stable | Confidence holding | Trade normally |

**CRITICAL**: "Rapidly eroding" while signal is STRONG_BUY = bull trap setting up.

#### Box 3: Levels (Macro Structure)
```
Levels: healthy · Break Risk 25%
```

| Status | Meaning | Trader Action |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| healthy | Room to run | Add to position |
| tested | Price near support/resistance | Prepare for break |
| Break Risk 25% | Low | Safe to hold |
| Break Risk 65%+ | High | Tight stop, ready to exit |

**Action**: If "tested" + "Break Risk 65%"  set stop just below level.

#### Box 4: Order Flow
```
Order Flow: strong_alignment
```

| Status | Meaning | Trader Action |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| strong_alignment | All signals agree | Confident add position |
| mild_alignment | Most signals agree | Normal position |
| neutral | Mixed signals | Wait for clarity |
| warning_divergence | Some conflict | Reduce position |
| critical_divergence | Major conflict | 🚨 EXIT, opposite developing |

**Key Rule**: At support/resistance, whoever breaks first wins. Don't get caught holding through a break.

## Best Practices

### ✅ DO:

1. **Trust RED Risk warnings** — They catch reversals
   - When you see RED + REVERSAL, reduce position immediately
   
2. **Use structure + confidence together**
   - At levels with "tested" status → use tight stops
   - Healthy levels → can hold longer

3. **Watch confidence velocity direction**
   - improving = add to existing position
   - eroding = reduce position
   - rapidly_eroding = EXIT

4. **Set mechanical stops at structure levels**
   - If at support: stop just below
   - If at resistance: stop just above

5. **Trade with momentum strength**
   - ACCELERATING > DECELERATING > STABLE
   - Don't add when momentum is decelerating

### ❌ DON'T:

1. **Ignore REVERSAL + HIGH RISK** — This is your reversal warning
2. **Add positions when confidence is eroding** — You're buying weakness
3. **Ignore "tested" level status** — Structure breaks are violent
4. **Trade against strong_alignment signals** — That's when institutions are sure
5. **Hold through order flow divergence**— When volume disagrees with direction, conflict is coming

## Timing Rules

### When to Enter

- ✅ GREEN + improving confidence + accelerating momentum
- ✅ After pullback that holds support level
- ✅ On momentum breakout (if not at resistance)

### When to Exit

- 🛑 RED + HIGH RISK appears
- 🛑 Confidence rapidly eroding (drop of 3+ per cycle)
- 🛑 At resistance with break risk > 60%
- 🛑 Structure breaks (price closes below/above tested level)

### When to Wait

- ⏸️ AMBER/MEDIUM risk + indecisive structure
- ⏸️ Confidence velocity at zero (flat)
- ⏸️ Order flow divergence (mixed signals)

## FAQ for Traders

**Q: Why does it show REVERSAL if current signal is STRONG_BUY?**

A: Because confidence is collapsing. Institutions are BUYING but losing conviction = bull trap. Price usually reverses 5-15 min later as the trap triggers.

---

**Q: Can I trade against the standard signal using Advanced 5m?**

A: Yes! This is the core feature. When Advanced 5m shows REVERSAL despite STRONG_BUY, that's a SHORT setup.

---

**Q: How many points can I expect?**

A: 0.3% to 1.5% moves (9-45 pts on NIFTY), depending on volatility. More in volatile markets, less in quiet markets.

---

**Q: What's the minimum hold time?**

A: 2-3 minutes to confirm prediction. Don't scalp milliseconds — wait for 2-3 5-min candles.

---

**Q: Should I override the signal for Advanced prediction?**

A: Yes, when HIGH RISK appears, reduce regardless of signal direction.

---

**Q: Does this work intraday only?**

A: Yes, designed for 5-15 minute trading only. Longer timeframes use Pure Liquidity Intelligence instead.
